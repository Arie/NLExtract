3D Apps with Dutch Open GeoData

Various web apps to demonstrate and use 3D web mapping.

Open Topo

OpenTopo in Cesium3D Viewer - viewer with OpenTopo 800px/km tiles as imagery layer in CesiumJS by Just van den Broecke

Top10NL3D

Top10NL3D on OpenTopo in Cesium3D Viewer - viewer with OpenTopo 800px/km and 3DBuildings from Top10NL3D (Valkenburg) by Just van den Broecke

Navigation in examples: 


    Panning
    Mouse Left click-hold + drag

    Zooming
    Right click-hold + drag, or
    Mouse wheel scroll

    Rotating/tilting
    Middle click-hold + drag, or
    CTRL + Left click-hold + drag
