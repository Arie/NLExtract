3D Apps with Dutch Open GeoData

Various web apps to demonstrate and use 3D web mapping. by Just van den Broecke

Open Topo

OpenTopo in Cesium3D Viewer - viewer with OpenTopo 800px/km tiles as imagery layer in CesiumJS

Ditto with Bing background - viewer with OpenTopo 800px/km tiles as imagery layer in CesiumJS

Top10NL3D

Top10NL3D on OpenTopo in Cesium3D Viewer - viewer with OpenTopo 800px/km and 3DBuildings from Top10NL3D (Valkenburg)

Top10NL3D on OpenTopo in OpenLayers3+Cesium3D Integrated - ditto, but using OL3-Cesium integration

Navigation in examples: 


    Panning
    Mouse Left click-hold + drag

    Zooming
    Right click-hold + drag, or
    Mouse wheel scroll

    Rotating/tilting
    Middle click-hold + drag, or
    CTRL + Left click-hold + drag
